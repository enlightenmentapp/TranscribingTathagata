Tathagata QA while traveling the USA

August 15 2011, 12:28 PM  by The Teaching of Tathagata

Q: Can Mr. Lee tell us a little more about what he does?

A: What I usually do is that I like to meet a variety of people, and I listen to them. I take their knowledge, and try to see the society from their eyesight as well. From those knowledge, I try to find some solution and alternative for the society. In some cases when people ask me about the solutions and answers, I also give them for their better life and better society.
